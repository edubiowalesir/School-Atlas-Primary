🌊 Five Oceans of the World

🌊 Pacific Ocean Largest ocean — covers 30% of Earth's surface. Deeper than all continents are tall!
🌊 Atlantic Ocean 2nd largest ocean. Lies between the Americas and Europe/Africa.
🌊 Indian Ocean Named after India! Surrounds India on three sides. 3rd largest ocean.
🌊 Arctic Ocean Smallest and coldest ocean. Covered with ice most of the year.
🌊 Southern Ocean Surrounds Antarctica. Recognized as the 5th ocean in year 2000.

🌍 Quick World Facts

🗺️ 7 Continents Africa, Asia, Europe, North America, South America, Australia, Antarctica
🌐 195 Countries 193 UN member states + Vatican City + Palestine = 195 total
🏔️ Highest Mountain Mount Everest — 8,848 m above sea level (Nepal/China border)
🔥 Hottest Place Furnace Creek, Death Valley, USA — recorded 56.7°C (134°F)
❄️ Coldest Place Vostok Station, Antarctica — recorded −89.2°C (−128.6°F)
👥 Most Populous India is the world's most populous country with over 1.4 billion people
