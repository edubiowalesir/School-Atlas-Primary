Major Rivers of India

भारत की प्रमुख नदियाँ

🌊
Ganga (Ganges)
गंगा
Length: ~2,525 km • Originates at Gangotri Glacier, Uttarakhand • Flows through UP, Bihar, West Bengal • Most sacred river of India
🌊
Yamuna
यमुना
Length: ~1,376 km • Originates at Yamunotri Glacier • Largest tributary of Ganga • Flows through Delhi, Agra (Taj Mahal is on its banks!)
🌊
Brahmaputra
ब्रह्मपुत्र
Length: ~2,900 km • Originates in Tibet • Flows through Assam • One of the widest rivers in the world
🌊
Godavari
गोदावरी
Length: ~1,465 km • Longest river in Peninsular India • Originates in Nashik, Maharashtra • Called "Dakshin Ganga" (Ganga of the South)
🌊
Krishna
कृष्णा
Length: ~1,400 km • Originates at Mahabaleshwar, Maharashtra • Flows through Telangana and Andhra Pradesh
🌊
Narmada
नर्मदा
Length: ~1,312 km • Originates in Madhya Pradesh • Flows into Arabian Sea • Divides North and South India
🌊
Indus
सिंधु
Length: ~3,180 km (in India: ~1,114 km) • Originates in Tibet • The Indus Valley Civilisation grew on its banks!
🌊
Tapti (Tapi)
ताप्ती
Length: ~724 km • Flows from east to west like Narmada • Originates in Madhya Pradesh, flows through Maharashtra and Gujarat
🌊
Kaveri (Cauvery)
कावेरी
Length: ~800 km • Known as the "Ganga of South India" • Originates in Kodagu, Karnataka • Forms beautiful waterfalls at Shivanasamudra
🌊
Mahanadi
महानदी
Length: ~858 km • Means "Great River" • Originates in Chhattisgarh • Flows through Odisha into Bay of Bengal
🌊
Sutlej
सतलुज
Length: ~1,450 km • One of the five rivers of Punjab • Originates in Tibet • Bhakra Dam is built on Sutlej
🌊
Son
सोन
Length: ~784 km • Major right-bank tributary of Ganga • Originates in Amarkantak, Madhya Pradesh

Major Rivers of the World

विश्व की प्रमुख नदियाँ

🌊
Nile
नील नदी
Length: 6,650 km • World's longest river • Flows through Egypt and Sudan into Mediterranean Sea • Ancient Egyptian civilisation grew on its banks
🌊
Amazon
अमेज़न नदी
Length: 6,400 km • World's largest river by water flow • Flows through South America (Brazil) • Surrounded by the world's largest rainforest!
🌊
Yangtze
यांग्त्ज़ी नदी
Length: 6,300 km • Longest river in Asia • Flows through China • Three Gorges Dam is built on Yangtze
🌊
Mississippi-Missouri
मिसिसिपी-मिसौरी
Length: 6,275 km • Largest river system in North America • Flows through the USA into Gulf of Mexico
🌊
Yenisei
येनिसी नदी
Length: 5,539 km • Largest river system in Russia • Flows into the Arctic Ocean
🌊
Congo
कांगो नदी
Length: 4,700 km • Deepest river in the world • Flows through central Africa • Surrounded by the Congo Rainforest
🌊
Volga
वोल्गा नदी
Length: 3,530 km • Longest river in Europe • Flows through Russia into Caspian Sea • Called "Mother Volga" by Russians
🌊
Niger
नाइजर नदी
Length: 4,180 km • Third longest river in Africa • Flows through West Africa, including Nigeria
🌊
Danube
डेन्यूब नदी
Length: 2,860 km • Second longest river in Europe • Flows through 10 countries including Austria, Hungary, Romania
🌊
Rhine
राइन नदी
Length: 1,230 km • Important river in Europe • Flows through Switzerland, Germany and Netherlands into North Sea
🌊
Thames
टेम्स नदी
Length: 346 km • River of London, UK • Tower Bridge and Big Ben are on its banks • Most famous river of England
🌊
Murray-Darling
मरे-डार्लिंग
Length: 3,750 km • Longest river system in Australia • Very important for farming in Australia
