7 Wonders of the World

दुनिया के सात अजूबे

Incredible structures built by humans across the world!

इंसानों द्वारा बनाए गए अद्भुत निर्माण!

🗿

The New Seven Wonders • नए सात अजूबे

2007 में दुनिया भर के लोगों ने मतदान करके इन्हें चुना

1

🇨🇳 Great Wall of China

चीन की महान दीवार

The longest wall in the world! It stretches over 21,000 km across northern China. Built to protect ancient China from enemies. It took hundreds of years and millions of workers to build.

📍 China • चीन
2

🇮🇳 Taj Mahal

ताज महल

A beautiful white marble palace in Agra, India. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is a symbol of love and one of the finest examples of Mughal architecture.

📍 Agra, India • आगरा, भारत
3

🇧🇷 Christ the Redeemer

क्राइस्ट द रिडीमर

A giant statue of Jesus Christ standing 38 metres tall on top of Mount Corcovado in Rio de Janeiro. It overlooks the entire city and is a symbol of peace and welcome.

📍 Rio de Janeiro, Brazil • ब्राज़ील
4

🇵🇪 Machu Picchu

माचू पिच्चू

An ancient Inca city high up in the Andes Mountains of Peru. Built around 1450, it was hidden in the clouds for centuries. It has temples, palaces, and homes made of perfectly fitted stones.

📍 Peru • पेरू
5

🇲🇽 Chichen Itza

चिचेन इत्ज़ा

A great Maya city in Mexico with a famous step pyramid called El Castillo. The pyramid has 365 steps — one for each day of the year! Built by the Maya civilisation over 1,000 years ago.

📍 Mexico • मेक्सिको
6

🇮🇹 Roman Colosseum

रोमन कोलोसियम

The largest ancient amphitheatre ever built, located in Rome, Italy. Completed in 80 AD, it could hold up to 80,000 spectators who came to watch gladiator battles and other events.

📍 Rome, Italy • रोम, इटली
7

🇯🇴 Petra

पेट्रा

An ancient city in Jordan carved directly into rose-red rock cliffs. It was built by the Nabataean people over 2,000 years ago. Petra is also called the "Rose City" because of the colour of its stone.

📍 Jordan • जॉर्डन
🏛️ Bonus Ancient Wonder! • प्राचीन आश्चर्य The Great Pyramid of Giza (Egypt 🇪🇬) is the only surviving wonder of the original Seven Wonders of the Ancient World! गीज़ा का महान पिरामिड!
