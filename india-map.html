🇮🇳 India at a Glance

🏛️ Capital New Delhi (नई दिल्ली) — on the banks of River Yamuna
📊 States & UTs 28 States + 8 Union Territories = 36 total administrative units
🏔️ Highest Peak Kangchenjunga — 8,586 m (3rd tallest in the world), Sikkim border
🗓️ Independence Day 15 August 1947 — India gained independence from British rule
🌊 Longest River Ganga — 2,525 km, flowing from Gangotri glacier to Bay of Bengal
🌡️ Climate Zones India has all major climate zones — tropical, arid, alpine and temperate
👥 Population 1.4+ billion people — India is the world's most populous country
🗣️ Languages India has 22 officially recognised languages and hundreds of dialects

🏅 National Symbols of India • राष्ट्रीय प्रतीक

🐯 National Animal Bengal Tiger (बाघ) — Project Tiger started in 1973 to protect it
🦚 National Bird Indian Peacock (मोर) — known for its beautiful colourful feathers
🌸 National Flower Lotus (कमल) — it is the symbol of purity and beauty
🌳 National Tree Banyan Tree (बरगद) — also called the 'wish-fulfilling tree'
🥭 National Fruit Mango (आम) — India is the largest producer of mangoes in the world!
🦬 National Aquatic Animal Gangetic River Dolphin (गांगेय डॉल्फिन) — found in the River Ganga
